Welcome to Clean Nest

What We Do

According to litteritcostsyou.org, the most common item found during clean-ups is fast food litter. Suburban areas have welcomed massive development in the past century. Allowing the fast food industry to populate nearly every community. Because of this, litter has been on the rise. Our goal is to help relieve the growing problem.

Where We Clean

Our main focuses are...

  • Main road sidewalks
  • Biking trails
  • Highway sides
  • Public parks
